Advanced Linear Algebra: Foundations to Frontiers

AI looks like magic, but underneath it's mostly linear algebra. Every neural network is a stack of matrix multiplications, transformers like ChatGPT and Claude reduce to three matrix products called "attention," and GPUs exist mainly to do this math at massive scale. The "intelligence" comes from scale and data — but the engine doing all the work is matrices and vectors.
