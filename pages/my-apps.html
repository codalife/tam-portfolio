Claw for Business Tax

TP Audit Defense

At the AI Engineer World’s Fair Hackathon, I built a self-improving AI voice agent — in 2 days — and made it a replica of me. When a tax authority challenges a company’s position, she builds the case to defend it.

The interesting part is how she learns. I don’t write her playbook — I coach her by video calls, the way you’d train a junior associate. Every voice lesson becomes a structured rule she stores in memory and feeds back into her own prompt. In parallel, an independent LLM judge scores every defense she writes against a fixed rubric, so I can watch her get measurably stronger each round.

What I didn’t expect: the rules she learned started generalizing to cases I never taught her.

Built on LiveKit + Gemini, with a Tavus avatar so she looks like me — I couldn’t get the voice cloning working in time, so that’s first on the list for v2. (For the hackathon, the tax knowledge base was AI-generated from the perspective of a global fintech company.)
